About Chebasas

THE BUILDERS AFRICA TRUSTS

Chebasas East Africa Limited was founded in 2021 with one vision — to transform the way East Africa builds. Registered under the Companies Act 2015 (Reg. No. PVT-DLULP3E8) and headquartered in Eldoret, Uasin Gishu County, we bring engineering precision and creative excellence to every project — from modest family homes to sprawling commercial complexes, from smooth highways to iconic bridges.

Led by Directors Derrick Kipsang Kipkemboi and Millsort Jeptoo Kemboi, every structure we raise is a promise kept — to our clients, to the community, and to the future generations who will live, work and travel through what we build today.
